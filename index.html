The Canvas Edge is a small, woman owned business selling original art by artist Isabel Carter. She usually creates artwork in her chosen mediums: acrylic paint on canvas (sometimes this will include a texture gel or modeling gel); pencil, pen, colored pencil and watercolors on paper; digital art created in Adobe Photoshop or Illustrator. All artwork will come with a signature, a date of completion and any other relevant information pertaining to the purchased piece. All physical pieces of art come varnished. She can create abstract art, realistic people, animals, is experimenting with landscapes and other artistic styles including, but not limited to, crochet and crafts falling into the contemporary arts category. The Canvas Edge is mainly focused on the sale of Isabel's visual and graphic art at this time.

The Gallery page has a selection of previous art work made that is not for sale. Learn more about the artist on the About Me page. Isabel's current works of art available for purchase can be found on the Store page. Here at The Canvas Edge, we try to make and/or carry something for everyone while maintaining the artist's own personal style. If you would like to contact her for any reason, you can do so via the Contact Me Page. Thank you for visiting us today; your support is greatly appreciated.
